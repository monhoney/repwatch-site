RepWatch Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

RepWatch ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Workout records: Date, exercise name, sets, reps, and weight entered by the user. Stored locally on your device (UserDefaults, private CloudKit container) only.
  • Body metrics: Height, weight, age, and gender entered during onboarding. Never transmitted off-device.
  • Purchase status: Whether the premium in-app purchase has been unlocked. Processed via Apple StoreKit — the developer has no access to payment details.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on your device or in your personal iCloud account (private CloudKit container). No data is sent to any developer-operated server.

4. Apple Watch ↔ iPhone Sync

Workout data and premium status are transferred between your devices using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework — device-to-device only, no external server involved.

5. In-App Purchases

Premium feature purchases are processed entirely through the Apple App Store. The developer never receives payment details. Purchases can be restored via your Apple ID.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact
